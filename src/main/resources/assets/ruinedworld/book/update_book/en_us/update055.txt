Update 0.5.5.BETA is the first update of the bigger update "The Scrap Update".
This update adds a few things to the game:
<NEWLINE>
The book you're currently reading, the Guide Book (Big work-in-progress),
Scrap, Bunch Of Scrap, Metal Pipe, Road Sign,
A new structure; Scrap Pallet and the Gas Barrel
<NEWLINE>
Metal Pipes and Road Signs can be smelted into Bunch Of Scrap which can be crafted into two Scraps.
Those can be smelted again into Scrap Metal which currently doesn't hold a use.
<NEWLINE>
There's one change:
The Oil Barrel's model has changed into a 3D Model.
<NEWLINE>
Translations remain to be undone.
